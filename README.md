📊 Talent Intelligence Dashboard — TA Analytics Maturity System
AI-powered Talent Acquisition Intelligence System enabling Descriptive → Diagnostic → Predictive → Prescriptive decision-making
________________________________________
🔹 Problem Statement
Most Talent Acquisition teams operate with fragmented dashboards that:
•	Show data but not decisions
•	Lack visibility into end-to-end hiring funnel performance
•	Do not connect TA metrics to business outcomes
•	Fail to identify root causes behind hiring inefficiencies
•	Are limited to descriptive reporting (what happened)
As a result:
•	Leadership lacks clarity on why hiring targets are missed
•	Recruiters optimize activity, not outcomes
•	TA functions struggle to evolve into strategic business partners
________________________________________
🔹 Solution
This project is an AI-powered Talent Intelligence Dashboard built as a decision system, not just a reporting layer.
It integrates:
•	TA Metrics Framework (Strategy Layer)
•	Funnel Analytics & Conversion Intelligence
•	Root Cause Analysis (RCA)
•	Predictive Hiring Signals
•	Prescriptive Action Recommendations
👉 Designed using system thinking, this dashboard moves beyond reporting into:
“What happened → Why it happened → What will happen → What should we do”

🔹 Key Insights from the Dashboard
Based on analysis of Info2026 Company data:
🚨 Critical Issues Identified
•	Severely low Fill Rate (~4.8%)
o	Indicates systemic inefficiency in conversion
•	Offer Acceptance Gap (~63% vs 82% target)
o	High risk of offer drop-offs / candidate experience issues
•	Funnel Bottleneck (L1 → L2 Conversion ~24%)
o	Major drop-off point → screening quality / role mismatch
•	High Agency Dependency (~44% of pipeline)
o	Low ROI (~0.7% hire rate)
•	High Position Drop Rate (~22.6%)
o	Indicates poor workforce planning / role clarity issues
________________________________________
🟢 Positive Signals
•	Strong Top-of-Funnel Volume
o	Candidate supply is not the issue
•	High Referral Efficiency (~9.1%)
o	~13x better than agency performance
•	Time-to-Fill within SLA (~21.8 days)
o	Process speed is strong → conversion is the problem
________________________________________
🧠 Strategic Interpretation
The system is activity-rich but outcome-poor
•	Volume ✅
•	Speed ✅
•	Conversion ❌
•	Quality ❌
👉 Core problem:
“Not a supply issue — a funnel quality and decision issue”
